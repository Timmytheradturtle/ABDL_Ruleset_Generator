Base Rules

Main Goal
  • You have to serve all of your Diaper Time (which can be found in the Variables table below). Once you have served all of it, the game is over (unless you have rules in your ruleset that state otherwise).
    • Note that there are some rules which may require you to add to your overall Diaper Time during the game, so make sure you keep track of any additions you may incur.
Base Tasks
  • Every hour of diaper time served, you must drink your entire Drinking Amount.
  • Every hour of diaper time served, you must suck on your paci for the amount of time specified by your Paci Time variable.
    • If you fail to do either, then
      • you must add 1 rule if you failed one of them or add 2 rules if you failed both,
      • time doesn't count as Diaper Time served until you've finished both,
      • you may not start your next random timer until you've finished both,
      • for each further hour spent without finishing your drinking/paci time, you must add an additional 2 rules for each one that is still not finished.
Roll Tables
  • Every 30 minutes, you must set a random timer for between 0 & 30 minutes.
    Whenever your timer goes off, you must roll on the Random Roll Table.
    • If you're wet when the timer goes off, you must also roll on the Soggy Roll Table.
    • If you're sucking on your paci or baby bottle when the timer goes off, you must also roll on the Paci Roll Table.
  • Whenever you wet your diaper, you must roll on the Wetting Roll Table.
  • Before you change your diaper, you must roll on the Changing Roll Table.
Other Rules
  • Every time you break a rule, you must add one more rule to your ruleset and abide by it for the remainder of the game.
  • Read all "Active Rules"! The "Active conditions" sidebar doesn't list all active rules, only the ones that have a trigger you have to pay attention to. The rule IDs not handled automatically are highlighted, but read the details of all the active rules before starting!
  • You may pause the game for any reason (for example to eat comfortably) at the cost of rolling 2 additional rules. You are still not allowed to remove your diaper(s) during the pause, and may only remove or cover any clothing/accessories you have on if you absolutely need to. Try to not pause during any random timers. Nothing you do and no time counts towards the game during the pause.
  • You are not allowed to change out of your current diaper until you believe you have saturated it enough to meet your Change Requirement. Your Change Requirement can be found below in the Variables table, and is measured by percentage of your diaper's max saturation (i.e. 100% saturation means your diaper is on the verge of leaking).
    • If you stay in a messy diaper for 30 minutes, then you may ignore your required saturation percentage and change (once you've also fulfilled any additional requirements imposed by your rules).
  • Corner time (some rules or rolls may direct you to do corner time) must be done with
    • holding a coin with your nose against a flat, vertical surface,
    • without touching the surface at any other point, and
    • without leaning against the surface, so there shouldn't be more than necessary pressure on the nose (a little here and there is fine, just don't put the weight of your head on it on purpose).
    • If you're kneeling, use a pillow under your knees to protect them.
  • Spankings (some rules or rolls may direct you to do spanks) must be
    • on bare skin,
    • either right below the diaper on the back of the thighs or on the inner thighs, or, if you can easily pull your diaper down, on your bare bottom.
    • You must count out loud after each swat, and any you forget to count before the next swat doesn't count.
  • During this game, you must be diapered at all times. There is no requirement for what kind of diaper you must have on.
    • You may not remove your diaper for the duration of this dare except to change or to use the potty for number two if that's against your limits (nothing in this dare strictly requires messing).
    • If you use the potty for number two, pause the game, add the required two additional rules for the pause, and try to not pee. If you pee more than 1-2 small drops, roll on the wetting table after you unpause the game.
  • If you don't have a pacifier, then you may suck on something else in place of one in order to fulfill your Paci Time. Thumb, favorite stuffie, even a bottle would technically count. However, if you do have a paci, then you are required to use it, and no substitutions are allowed.
  • If your random timer goes off while you are being spanked or in corner time, you must wait to perform your required rolls until after you are finished with all spankings & corner time you have assigned.
